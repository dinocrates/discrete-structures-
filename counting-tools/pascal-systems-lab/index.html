1. The Two Parents

Separate length-6, weight-3 strings by their first bit. Why do the groups add to 20?

Show conclusion

C(6,3) = C(5,2) + C(5,3) = 10 + 10 = 20.

2. Same Outcome, New Representation

Trace {B,D}, 01010, and R U R U R across the panels.

Show conclusion

Every representation records the same five yes/no positions, so the conversion is one-to-one.

3. Include or Exclude

Compare three enabled systems with five disabled systems.

Show conclusion

C(8,3) = C(8,5) = 56 through complementary choices.

4. Count Every Subset

Add row 5 and compare it with every length-5 bit string.

Show conclusion

1 + 5 + 10 + 10 + 5 + 1 = 32 = 2⁵.
